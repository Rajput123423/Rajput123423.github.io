ABOUT KATIHAR

A beautiful tourist destination filled with mountains, rivers, and lakes. Its proximity to the Himalayas in the north, Jharkhand plateau in the south and the passage of holy river Ganga makes it a pleasurable spot for travelers. Katihar is famous for its Jute mills. The city is full of heritage & culture and closely linked with the parent district of Purnea.

GORAKHNATH TEMPLE

Temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva, thousands of devotees come here for their prayers to Lord Shiva. The temple is decorated with flowers and ornaments inside the temple. One of the central attractions is the Shivalinga. Shaivites visit this place on the occasion of Mahashivratri to celebrate it.

BAGI MATH

Baghi Math is full of intense devotion, spirituality, and faith of the people around. It is an amazing place in Katihar and can never be believed until or unless you visit this beautiful religious destination. It is known for its 108 rooms which are built for different spiritual activities. Take a few days off to free your mind. Its a perfect place to enhance your spiritual levels.

GOGA LAKE

Goga Lake is one of the greatest wetlands wordpress of Bihar. It is spread over 217 acres of area. Goga lake attracts a huge number of tourists. The Lake links two rivers Ganga and Mahananda through tributaries during the rainy season. Various varieties of aquatic animals, terrestrial and rare species of birds can be found in this place. Lake is full of flora and fauna. Around 300 migratory birds come here in every monsoon and winters. For nature and wild life lovers, Goga Lake is a perfect place.

KALI MANDIR

The Temple has got an immense historical significance. Temple is dedicated to Goddess Kali. It is decorated beautifully from the inside, which is the best attraction for the visitors. The attractive sculptures on the walls show its archaeological history. During festivals like Durgashtami and Diwali, a huge number of devotees come here for Puja and festival celebrations in the temple.

RAMKISHNA MISSION ASHRAM

It was started in 1925, the Ashram was created for providing remedies to plague and malaria patients. It gradually increased its complex and services and now it becomes a small holy place. The center has flourished itself to be one of the most active centers of Ramakrishna Mission. It explains the principles of ancient Vedanta in the modern language. Devotees from all over India visit Ramakrishna Ashram.
